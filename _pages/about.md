# Professional Overview

Dr. Syed Muntasir MAMUN is the Chief Innovation Officer and Director General of International Trade, Investment & Technology at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bangladesh. With over 20 years of distinguished service in Foreign Service and Diplomacy, he has established himself as an expert in South and East Asian politics and political economy.

## Career Highlights

* Author of "Blockchain: Gaming & Collusion - A Reading in Political Economy" ([Amazon Author Page](https://www.amazon.com/author/syedmuntasir))
* Pioneered the first Neural Network (Artificial Intelligence) Lab in MOFA, Bangladesh
* 20+ years of service in Foreign Service and Diplomacy
* Expert in South and East Asian politics and political economy
* Specialized in High Profile Client Development and Management (HPCDM)

## Current Initiatives
