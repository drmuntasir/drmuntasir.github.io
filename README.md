# Dr. Syed Muntasir MAMUN's Academic Portfolio

This repository hosts my academic and professional portfolio website, accessible at [drmuntasir.github.io](https://drmuntasir.github.io). The site showcases my work as Chief Innovation Officer and Director General at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bangladesh, along with my academic contributions and professional achievements.

## Portfolio Highlights

- **Professional Experience**: 20+ years in Foreign Service and Diplomacy
- **Innovation Leadership**: Establishing the first Neural Network Lab in MOFA
- **Publications**: Author of "Blockchain: Gaming & Collusion - A Reading in Political Economy"
- **Digital Transformation**: Leading comprehensive digital initiatives across 81 diplomatic missions
